Nikola Tesla

A Serbian-American inventor, electrical engineer
mechanical engineer and futurist who contributed to the
design of the modern alternating current electricity supply

A Timeline of Nikola Tesla's Life:

  • 1859 - Born in Smiljan, Croatia
  • 1870 - Tesla faced a near-death circumstances
  • 1875 - Tesla enrolls in college
  • 1880 - Tesla moves to prague to study philosophy
  • 1881 - Tesla begins his career as chief electical engineer
  • 1882 - Tesla goes to work for Edison
  • 1885 - Tesla submits some of his first patents
  • 1887 - Tesla re-imagines the electric motor
  • 1890 - Tesla begins is work on wireless power transmission
  • 1891 - Tesla Patents the tesla coil
  • 1943 - Tesla death
